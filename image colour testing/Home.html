Video: hangover

"Hangover" is a single by South Korean rapper Psy, featuring American rapper Snoop Dogg. Produced by Yoo Gun-hyung, Its music video debuted on the June 8, 2014, broadcast of Jimmy Kimmel Live!.

As of June 2023, the video has received over 362 million views on YouTube. The song serves as a follow-up to Psy's two international hit singles "Gangnam Style" and "Gentleman".

The song was used in the 2018 Marvel superhero film Black Panther during the Busan casino scene.
